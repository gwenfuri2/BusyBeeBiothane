About Us

Welcome to Busy Bee Biothane! We are a passionate team dedicated to crafting BioThane products for service dogs with exceptional quality and cuteness.

Our goal is to provide service dog handlers with reliable gear that offers superior water resistance, easy maintenance, and a lightweight design. We believe that service dogs deserve gear that not only performs well but also looks adorable!

From stylish collars to durable leashes, our products are designed with both functionality and cuteness in mind. We understand the importance of a strong bond between service dogs and their handlers, and our gear aims to enhance that connection.

Thank you for choosing Busy Bee Biothane. We are excited to be a part of your service dog's journey and contribute to their safety, comfort, and style.
